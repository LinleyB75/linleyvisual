Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

At LinleyB.com, operated by Linley B, the privacy of my prospective students and visitors is highly respected. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data collected by this website and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

I only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to me. This occurs when you:

  • Submit an inquiry through the contact form (Name, Email Address, and your English learning goals).

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

The information collected is used strictly in the following circumstances:

  • To respond to your direct inquiries and facilitate communication regarding English lessons.
  • To analyze overall anonymous website traffic and improve user experience via analytics tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

I utilize trusted third-party platforms to ensure the secure operation of this website. These services have their own strict privacy policies:

  • FormSubmit: My contact form is powered by FormSubmit. When you send a message, the data is securely routed through their servers directly to my private inbox.
  • Google Analytics: I use Google Analytics to monitor website traffic. Google Analytics collects anonymized data (such as browser types and pages visited) using cookies.

4. Cookies

This website uses cookies (small text files placed on your device) to store information about visitors' preferences, such as your Light/Dark mode setting, and to track anonymous website usage via Google Analytics. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

I take the security of your personal data seriously and implement reasonable technical measures to protect against the unauthorized access or destruction of your information.

6. Your Rights

If you would like to request the deletion of your contact form data or any past email communication regarding tutoring, please email me directly.

7. Contact Details

If you require any more information or have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me directly at: blanyves@optusnet.com.au.
