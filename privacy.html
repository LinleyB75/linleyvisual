Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

At LinleyB.com, operated by Linley Bignoux, the privacy of our visitors and clients is of extreme importance. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data collected by this website and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide. This occurs when you:

  • Submit a project inquiry through the contact form (Name, Email Address, and Project Details).
  • Subscribe to the "Email Dev Insights" newsletter (Email Address).

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

The information collected is used in the following circumstances:

  • To respond to your direct inquiries, provide development quotes, and facilitate client communication.
  • To send periodic newsletters containing technical tips and updates (only if you have explicitly opted in).
  • To analyze overall website traffic and improve user experience via anonymous analytics.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party platforms to ensure the secure and smooth operation of this website. These services act as data processors and have their own strict privacy policies:

  • FormSubmit: Our contact form is powered by FormSubmit. When you send a message, the data is securely routed through their servers directly to my private inbox.
  • MailerLite: We use MailerLite to manage our email newsletter subscriber list and send campaigns. If you subscribe, your email address is securely stored within the MailerLite platform.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to monitor website traffic. Google Analytics collects anonymized data (such as browser types and pages visited) using cookies.

4. Cookies

This website uses cookies (small text files placed on your device) to store information about visitors' preferences, such as your Light/Dark mode setting, and to track anonymous website usage via Google Analytics. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement reasonable administrative and technical security measures to protect against the unauthorized access or destruction of your information.

6. Your Rights (Opt-Out & Deletion)

If you have subscribed to the newsletter, you may opt-out at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link located at the bottom of any email you receive from us. If you would like to request the deletion of your contact form data, please email me directly.

7. Contact Details

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact me directly by email at: blanyves@optusnet.com.au.
