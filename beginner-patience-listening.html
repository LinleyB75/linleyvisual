Patience & Listening for Beginners

If you are a beginner (A1-A2) or returning to English after many years, you might feel frustrated. You want to speak fluently now, but the words come out slowly.

This is normal. Learning a language is not a sprint; it is a marathon.

The Power of Listening

Before you can speak well, you must listen well. Many beginners try to force speaking before they have heard enough English.

  • Action: Listen to simple English podcasts for 10 minutes every day.
  • Action: Watch YouTube videos with subtitles.
  • Action: Be patient with yourself. Small steps every day add up to big results.

In our beginner classes, we focus heavily on listening and repeating. We build your confidence slowly, so you feel safe to make mistakes and learn.
